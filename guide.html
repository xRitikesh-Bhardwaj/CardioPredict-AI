Medical Guide (Easy Hinglish)

Yeh guide aapko har input samajhne mein help karegi

Basic Health (BP, Cholesterol, Sugar)

Yeh aapke heart aur blood ki basic condition batata hai.

  • Blood Pressure (trestbps): Normal ~120 hota hai. 130+ ho to high BP ho sakta hai.
  • Cholesterol (chol): 200 se kam normal hai. 240+ dangerous ho sakta hai.
  • High Blood Sugar (fbs):
    ✔ 1 = Sugar high hai (diabetes risk)
    ❌ 0 = Normal

Chest Pain Type

Yeh batata hai chest pain kis type ka hai. Har pain heart problem nahi hota.

  • Typical Angina (0): Exercise ya kaam karte waqt pain hota hai, rest lene se thik ho jata hai.
  • Atypical Angina (1): Pain hota hai lekin clear pattern nahi hota.
  • Non-anginal Pain (2): Gas, acidity ya muscle pain (heart se related nahi).
  • Asymptomatic (3): Pain nahi hai, lekin heart risk ho sakta hai.

ECG Result

Yeh heart ka electrical test hai.

  • 0 = Normal: Sab sahi hai.
  • 1 = Minor Issue: Thodi abnormality hai.
  • 2 = Serious Issue: Heart problem ho sakti hai.

Exercise Data

Exercise ke time heart ka behaviour check hota hai.

  • Maximum Heart Rate (thalach): Exercise ke time heart kitna fast beat karta hai.
  • Exercise Pain (exang):
    ✔ 1 = Exercise ke time pain hota hai
    ❌ 0 = Pain nahi hota
  • Heart Stress Level (oldpeak): Exercise ke baad heart pe stress.
  • Heart Response (slope):
    ✔ 0 = Normal
    ❌ 1 = No change
    ⚠ 2 = Decreasing (problem ho sakti hai)

Advanced Tests

Yeh detailed tests hote hain jo heart blockage aur blood issues batate hain.

  • Blocked Vessels (ca):
    0 = No blockage
    1 = Mild
    2 = Moderate
    3 = Severe (dangerous)
  • Thalassemia (thal):
    ✔ 1 = Normal
    ❌ 2 = Fixed defect
    ⚠ 3 = Reversible defect
